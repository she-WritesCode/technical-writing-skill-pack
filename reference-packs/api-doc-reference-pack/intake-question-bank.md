# Intake Question Bank

Use these questions when the source inventory is weak.

## Product and audience

- Who is the primary reader: internal engineer, external developer, partner, or support team?
- What task is the reader trying to complete?
- What background can we assume?

## Behavior and scope

- What does this API or feature do in one sentence?
- What is explicitly in scope for this document?
- What is out of scope?
- What changed recently?

## Inputs and outputs

- What are the required inputs?
- What are the optional inputs?
- What fields are easy to misunderstand?
- What response shapes matter most to users?

## Constraints and caveats

- What auth or permission constraints apply?
- What rate limits, quotas, or pagination rules apply?
- What validation or error cases are common?
- What edge cases cause user confusion?

## Adoption and migration

- Is this new, updated, deprecated, or replacing something older?
- What would break for an existing integrator?
- Are there migration steps or compatibility notes?

## Examples

- What is the most representative request example?
- What is the most representative success response?
- What failure example would be most useful?

## Review routing

- Who can confirm factual accuracy?
- Who can confirm naming and terminology?
- Who can confirm examples reflect real usage?
